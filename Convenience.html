Everyday Automation

Explore the core features of your Smart Home, designed for ultimate convenience. Essential integrations streamline daily tasks, creating a harmonious and efficient living space.

Voice

The voice system delivers real-time updates through friendly, responsive voices. Receive announcements for the time, upcoming schedules, and important reminders, ensuring you never miss a beat. Whether its gentle prompt for your next appointment or a cheerful notification about the weather and date, the voice assistants keeps your day running smoothly.

Comfort

Imagine waking up to a perfectly cooked breakfast, courtesy of an intelligent stove that prepares your favorite meals at the precise time you desire. As you start your day, bed warmers gently heat your sheets, ensuring you wake up in a cozy embrace. When it's time to unwind, automated bath systems fill your tub with the ideal temperature and soothing scents, creating a tranquil oasis just for you.

Essentials

Start your day with real-time updates from the weather, a sleek device that provides daily forecasts, temperature information, and alerts, ensuring you're always prepared for the day ahead. Additionally, the automated garage doors offer seamless entry and exit, responding to your commands or schedules. Whether you're arriving home or heading out, these doors open and close effortlessly, adding convenience and security to your routine.

Outdoor Essentials

The intelligent garden sprinklers automatically adjust watering schedules based on weather conditions, ensuring your lawn and plants thrive with minimal effort. Meanwhile, your patio transforms at the touch of a button-patio tables emerge flawlessly from the walls, ready to serve drinks, food, and display cards for gatherings. Paired with built-in speakers that play your favorite music, your outdoor space becomes the ultimate setting for relaxation and entertainment.

Smart Cleaning Essentials

Let your home clean itself with these smart cleaning essentails. From robotic vacuums to self-operating dishwashers and incinerators, these features ensure your space is always pristine and clutter-free.

Dishwasher

The aluminum wedge is a versatile tool designed to scrape away through food residues before the dishes even enter the dishwasher. Its sleek durable design ensures no stubborn grime is left behind. Once prepared, the smart dishwasher takes over, offering powerful, automated cleaning with precision. It adjusts water temperature and pressure based on the load, ensuring spotless dishes every time.

Robot Mice & Rats

This Smart Home offers DustMice and CopperScrape Rats, the smart cleaning duo designed to keep your home spotless. DustMice, small robotic cleaners shaped like mice, swiftly move through your home, dusting surfaces and cleaning hard-to-reach places. Their compact size allows them to navigate tight spaces, ensuring every corner stays dust-free.

Meanwhile, the CopperScrap Rats, larger industrial-style robots, are designed to collect bigger pieces of trash and debris. Built with strong, durable copper exteriors, these rats transport waste directly to the incinerator for disposal, ensuring efficient and Eco-friendly waste management.

Incinerator

With a modern design, the incinerator discreetly operates within your home, automatically detecting and processing waste as it is deposited. The incinerator features advanced safety mechanisms and environmental controls, ensuring safe operation while minimizing emissions.
