Technology Features

Our Smart Home technology brings lightning, climate, security, and entertainment together, delivering personalized automation and hands-free control, all while optimizing energy use.

Automated Technology

Our advanced Smart Home automation system is designed to ideally manage every aspect of your daily life. From the moment you step inside, the house responds intuitively to your needs-automatically adjusting lightning, temperature, and ambiance to create the perfect environment.

The Brain

Step into a world where your home thinks of you. The ultimate brain of the Smart Home is engineered to bring your living space to life. This intelligent control center synchronizes every aspect of your home's technology. Your home adapts to your routines, creating custom experiences that enhance comfort and efficiency. With voice and app integration, it ensures effortless control while monitoring safety and energy usage.

Memory Tapes

The memory tapes are the ultimate archive for the Smart Home. These cutting-edge storage devices capture your home's daily activities, routines, and preferences, allowing your smart system to learn, adapt, and automate with unmatched precision. Designed to recall specific events, reminders, and tasks, they allow the Smart Home to anticipate your needs based on past interactions. Efficient, intuative, and timeless, memory tapes ensure that the Smart Home never forgets what matters most to you.

Self Protection

The Smart Home self protection system is created to safeguard the Smart Home with unparalleled precision. Equipped with password and sound recognition, it ensures access is granted only to trusted individuals through voice authentication which includes your dog's bark and secure codes. Automatic shudders and shades activate at thefirst sign of unwanted guests or animals, closing off windows and doors to create a fortified barrier. Sensors detect motion, and protective curtains seal the windows, keeping your home secure even in your absence. Rest easy knowing your Smart Home is always one step ahead when it comes to your safety.

Safety

Like a gentle breeze carrying the whispers of nature, our safety features provide unobtrusive yet essential protection. From fire alarms to flood sensors, your home remains a sanctuary, always ready to respond.

Fire Alarm & Water

Equipped with warning lights, voice, alarms, and conceled water systems, the fire system instantly detects and suppresses fires with pre-loaded water reserves hidden in your ceilings and walls. At the first sign of smoke or fire, the fire system activates a multi-sensory alarm system, flashing warning lights and issuing clear voice alerts to ensure immediate awareness. Hidden within your ceiling and walls are pre-charged water reserves, ready to deploy automatically. These concealed sprinklers release a controlled water spray to douse flames, preventing the spread of fire and minimizing damage.

Water Rats

Upon detecting smoke or heat, intelligent robots disguised as rats, emerge from hidden compartments and swiftly release powerful streams of water from their bult-in water pistols. Their precise aim targets the fire directly, extinguishing flames before they are spread. Designed to move through narrow spaces and reach high-risk areas, these robotic defenders ensure rapid, efficient fire control without causing unnecessary water damage.

Robots

Safeguard your home with an innovative fire supression system that activates in seconds. When a fire is detected, faucet-like mouth robots spring to life, spraying a specially formulated green fire retardant. This eco-friendly solution smothers flames on contact, reducing the spread of fire while minimizing damage to your home. These robots are strategically placed throughout your attic trapdoors and ceilings, ensuring they can reach any area at risk.
