Room Features

Smart Home technology transforms every room into a more functional and efficient space. The rooms and their technoly make the entire home more comfortable, secure, and efficient.

Study

Designed to enhance productivity and relaxation, this study room combines advanced technology with refined style to create the ultimate environment for focused work and serene moments.

Modern Fireplace

The centerpiece of the room, the sleek wall-mounted fireplace, adds both warmth and touch of sophistication. With smart controls, you can easily adjust the flame intensity and temperature to suit your mood, creating a cozy atmoshphere perfect for deep concentration or unwinding after a long day.

Ergonomic Chairs

The ergonomic chairs in this room give you the experience of unparalleled comfort. Designed for extended periods of use, these chairs offer customizable settings for lumbar support, tilt, and height adjustments. Upholstered in luxurious, breathable fabric, they provide both support and style, ensuring your work sessions are as comfortable as they are productive.

Intergrated Sound System

Elevate your study sessions with the state-of-the-art sound system embedded discreetly within the room. Whether you prefer calming classical music or an invigorating soundtrack, you can control the system effortlessly via voice commands or a sleek touch-screen panel. The crystal-clear audio enhances your focus and helps set the perfect mood.

Poetry Reader

This sophisticated device offers a curated selection of classic and contemporary poems read aloud with expressive clarity. Perfect for moments of inspiration or relaxation, it brings the beauty of poetry into your study routine with just a simple touch.

Nursery

Glass Walls

The nursery's standout feature is its interactive glass walls, which transform thre room into a dynamic, visual experience. These high-definition screens can display a variety of calming and engaging images, from soothing nature scenes to playful animations, enhancing your baby's visual stimulation. The images are easily customizable and can be controlled via a smart panel or mobile app, allowing you to adjust the visuals based on your baby's mood or development.

Media

The nursery includes touch-sensitive elements embedded in the furniture and walls. These features respond to gentle touches, providing a soothing sensation through textured surfaces and interactive panels. The tactile elements are designed to engage your baby's sense of touch, offering comforting textures and interactive experiences that stimulate sensory exploration.

The nursery also has an aromatic system that synchonizes scents with the displayed images. For instance, if the image is of a forest, the system might release subtle, fresh pine or earthy scents; if it's a beach scene, the aroma might be of sea breeze or coconut. This harmony between sight and smell creates a fully immersive sensory experience, promoting relaxation and exploration.
